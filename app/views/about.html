Weather Top 1000

Welcome to Weather Top 1000 - a modular weather station

To date this low cost device takes a set of meteorological readings and displays these on a simple LCD display. Until now !


This is a joint venture between WeatherTop Inc and GrantApps to bring Ireland's weather stations to a digital platform.

This is a prototype exhibition into the power that this application can achieve.

Right now we have two stations Dunmore & Tramore. The user is still manually inputting data read from the station, but in the future this data will become realtime, historized and continuous.
